About Francesca
Frankie

I'm a marketing & advertising professional with 8+ years of experience building creative solutions for big brands and clients across numerous marketing channels. I started my career on Target's social media team, engaging 1:1 with customers on Twitter, creating quick-turn content based on trending conversations, and writing creative briefs for campaigns rooted in social media insights.

Looking to expand my channel expertise, I pivoted to Amazon's advertising business to gain experience in client services and digital advertising. Combining my creative muscles flexed at Target and my client services skills from Amazon set me up for success as a Creative Strategist in Spotify's advertising business. I churned out hundreds of creative pitches in response to RFPs from clients of all industries.

As our organization evolved, my role at Spotify shifted from 1:1 RFP work to marketing Spotify Advertising at scale. I led B2B marketing campaigns targeting our largest client vertical, CPG, across paid media, email, gifting, sponsorships, events, editorial content, and sales collateral.

My current role at Spotify involves ideating and executing in-app marketing campaigns for our latest product launches and partnership collaborations. I love the variety of switching between launching our AI-powered DJ product and bringing our partnership with Netflix's Bridgerton to life.

When I'm not working, you'll find me attempting Julia Turshen's latest recipe, working on my pottery prowess (who needs a mug!), or walking along Lake Michigan with my partner.
