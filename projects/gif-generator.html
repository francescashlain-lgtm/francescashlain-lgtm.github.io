Project Detail

From facing the crowds in-store to shopping in slippers at home, everyone has their own way to participate in the biggest event of the year for Target: Black Friday. Let's celebrate each customer's personal journey with customized social content.

We built an internal tool filled with Target branded GIFs that can be quickly personalized to a customer, adding in their name or social media handle.

Using these personalized GIFs in our 1:1 social conversations on Black Friday increased social media engagement +9% and retweets +20% year-over-year.
