Reinventing Target's Instagram Strategy

In 2017, Instagram, once a platform for carefully crafted images, was becoming a destination for real-time posting and impulse shopping. Target's Instagram strategy, "where products come to play," included highly stylized imagery resulting in beautiful, but time-consuming and costly posts.

I developed a new Instagram strategy to mimic what our customers were sharing on Instagram: their Target finds. Our new strategy, "share the finds," focused on discovery and user-generated content, resulting in increased engagement on Instagram and decreased production costs.
