Sip & Sound: mixing now with nostalgia

Employer

Spotify

Project Scope

Copywriting 〰️ Sales Enablement 〰️ Event Production 〰️ Media Planning

Project Detail

Many alcohol brands take nostalgia to the next level, channeling old-school flavors and creative packaging of the past. At Spotify, we know that audio is particularly perfect for generating a trip down memory lane—in our research, we've seen that music is the #1 trigger for nostalgia.

We invited our top alcohol advertisers to a special event showcasing how their customers are mixing now with nostalgia on Spotify. We encouraged attendance through an article on our website, paid LinkedIn posts, and an enticing guest star: Bill Simmons.
