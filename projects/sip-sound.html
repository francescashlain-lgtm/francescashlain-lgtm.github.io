Sip & Sound: mixing now with nostalgia

Insight

In 2021, we've seen many alcohol brands take nostalgia to the next level, channeling old-school flavors and creative packaging of the past. At Spotify, we know that audio is particularly perfect for generating a trip down memory lane—in our research, we've seen that music is the #1 trigger for nostalgia.

Idea

Invite our top alcohol advertisers to a special event showcasing how their buyers are mixing now with nostalgia on Spotify. Encourage attendance through an article on our website, paid LinkedIn posts, and an enticing guest star: Bill Simmons.

Impact

  • 65% of event attendees downloaded our one-sheet to learn more
  • 55% of event attendees signed up for our newsletter
