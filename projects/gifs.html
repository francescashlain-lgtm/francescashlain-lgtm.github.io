A GIF is worth a thousand words

Employer

Target

Project Scope

Creative Strategy 〰️ Creative Production

Project Detail

From emojis to GIFs to videos, people are increasingly using visual tools on social media to express themselves and communicate with each other.

We created a library of Target branded GIFs based on conversation themes we see on social media. By uploading the GIFs to GIPHY, they can be used by our social engagement team in 1:1 conversations and by customers in their own text and social conversations.

Since 2016, Target has uploaded almost 800 GIFs to GIPHY and received 2.9 billion GIF views on GIPHY.
