Cheerios: Hacking Prime Day

Employer

Amazon

Client

General Mills

Project Scope

Media Planning 〰️ Creative Production

Project Detail

When voice shopping, 85% of customers select Amazon Alexa's recommendation. To ensure recommendations are useful, Alexa tends to tell shoppers about products they've bought before.

We leveraged America's biggest retail event, Amazon's Prime Day, to establish a Cheerios shopping history so the next time someone says, "Hey Alexa, add cereal to my shopping cart," Honey Nut Cheerios is the first recommendation. We designed a giveaway, offering a free family-size box of Honey Nut Cheerios to everyone who spent over $40 on Amazon during Prime Day. We promoted this offer during a Prime Day Live segment and on Fire Tablet, desktop, and mobile. All ad placements clicked through to a custom landing page, informing shoppers how to claim the deal.

Honey Nut Cheerios became the #1 grocery item on Prime Day 2018 in the U.S., with a sales increase of 64% vs. the week prior, year-over-year. 80% of the customers in July were new to the brand.
