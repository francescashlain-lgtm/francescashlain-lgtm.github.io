Culture Next: Spotify's annual global trend report

Insight

2020 was a cultural wake-up call. 2021 is a year for cultural rebirth.

Idea

Focus our annual global trend report, Culture Next, on how audio is playing a major part in this cultural rebirth for Gen Zs and millennials in three chapters: Listeners, Creators, and Advertisers. The "what it means for brands" section of each trend, which I wrote, interprets how brands can act on these trends through audio advertising on Spotify.

Impact

In comparison to the 2020 Culture Next campaign:

  • +77% increase in views to the interactive Culture Next microsite
  • +173% increase in new leads for Spotify Advertising
  • +249% increase in form fills to download the report
