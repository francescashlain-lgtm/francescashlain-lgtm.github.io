Culture Next: Spotify's annual global trend report

Employer

Spotify

Project Scope

Copywriting 〰️ Sales Enablement

Project Detail

2020 was a cultural wake-up call. 2021 is a year for cultural rebirth.

We focused Spotify Advertising's annual global trend report, Culture Next, on how audio is playing a major part in this cultural rebirth for Gen Zs and millennials in three chapters: Listeners, Creators, and Advertisers. The "what it means for brands" section of each trend, which I wrote, interprets how brands can act on these trends through audio advertising on Spotify.
