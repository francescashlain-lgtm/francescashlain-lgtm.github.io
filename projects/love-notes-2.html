Automating Target's all-star campaign

Insight

In 2016, we manually sent 1,000+ love notes to our customers who proclaimed "I love Target" with our campaign: #TargetLoveNotes. With an engagement rate 3x higher than our benchmark, the love was definitely mutual. However, this success was achieved through time-consuming, manual work.

Idea

Build a mini-site to allow our customers to send their love in a cool, personalized GIF. We created a tool that both our internal team and customers could use on Valentine's Day. The mini-site is intuitive and makes it so easy to share your GIF on any social channel you want. We used Twitter and three different Facebook carousels as our primary means to promote #TargetLoveNotes to the masses.

Impact

  • Over 65K personalized GIFs created from the mini-site
  • 34.9M impressions, 5M video views, and 621K Twitter engagements from our paid media
