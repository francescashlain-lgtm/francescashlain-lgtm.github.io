Automating Target's all-star campaign

Employer

Target

Project Scope

Social Media Strategy 〰️ Paid Media Planning 〰️ Creative Production

Project Detail

In 2016, we manually sent 1,000+ love notes to our customers who proclaimed "I love Target" with our campaign: #TargetLoveNotes. With an engagement rate 3x higher than our benchmark, the love was definitely mutual. However, this success was achieved through time-consuming, manual work.

We built a mini-site to allow our customers to send their love in a cool, personalized GIF. The tool was accessible to both our internal team and customers on Valentine's Day, making it easy to share GIFs across any social channel. We used Twitter and three different Facebook carousels as our primary means to promote #TargetLoveNotes to the masses.

Over 65K personalized GIFs were created from the mini-site, generating 34.9M impressions, 5M video views, and 621K Twitter engagements from our paid media.
